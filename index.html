Controls

Movement keys: W A S D or arrow keys
Attack 1: left clicking will attack in the direction you are currently facing.
Attack 2: Pressing the 1 key will attack in a circle around the character.
Mute Sound: Pressing the back quote (tilde ~) key will mute and unmute audio.

Information

Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
Internet Explorer is incompatible due to lack of ES6 support.
